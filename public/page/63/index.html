HODL

Brief Definition and Origin “HODL” is a term widely used within the cryptocurrency community, originating from a misspelled word for “hold.” It first appeared in a 2013 post on a Bitcoin forum, where a user, in the midst of a market crash, declared their intention to “hodl” rather than sell their Bitcoin. Since then, “HODL” has evolved beyond a simple typo into a full-fledged investment strategy among cryptocurrency enthusiasts. It refers to the act of holding onto cryptocurrency investments long-term, regardless of market volatility, with the belief that their value will significantly increase over time.
